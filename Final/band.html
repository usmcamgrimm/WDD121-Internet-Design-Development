Hail the Gods! Fenrir hails from the United States.
Formed in 2014 by Singer Guitar and Bass Player in Cleveland, OH, their first demo was recorded in January 2017. By March, they had been signed to Storm Surge Records. Their first release, "Fenrir" was released in May 2018.


  • Singer Guitar - Vocals / Guitar
  • Guitar Player - Guitar
  • Bass Player - Bass
  • Drum Drummer - Drums
